AI Integrity Guide

Flip the cards to learn how to use AI responsibly.

01. Prompt Hints

Think First

Ask for a logic step or a brainstorm, not the final answer. Keep the thinking yours.

02. Cross-Verify

Double Check

AI is a 'drafter' prone to errors. Always verify data with two reliable sources.

03. Disclose Use

Transparency

Be transparent. State where AI assisted your process to build trust with teachers.

04. Your Voice

Authenticity

AI content is a tool for ideas, but the final argument must sound like you.

05. Copyright

Originality

Do not let AI copy material verbatim. Give proper attribution to all sources.

06. Final Review

Editor-in-Chief

You are the final authority. Ensure the product meets your standards and ethics.
